Roots-The Education Mile

The Design for Education Conclave 2025 is a three-day immersive and travelling platform that brings together thought and practice, blending field immersion, hands-on learning, and powerful conversations.

Over two days in Nagapattinam and one day in Chennai, participants will explore how education, livelihoods, and policy intersect, not just through lectures, but through lived experience. You’ll learn from educators, policymakers, artisans, and community leaders, in classrooms, in villages, and by the sea.

From mending nets to shaping clay, engaging in cultural circles to attending focused lectures, every part of this journey is designed to spark grounded, contextual, and child-centric solutions.

Explore The Journey

Day - 1:
What Can a Village
Teach About Design?

📍 Kameswaram Village, Nagapattinam

Have you ever watched a community solve problems without using the word “design”?

Here, you’ll walk through boats and backwaters, rituals and routines and notice how learning is woven into life itself.

Themes: Community Wisdom • Adaptive Problem-Solving • Learning from Livelihoods

Day - 2:
Learn by Doing,
Listen by Being

📍 Pottery Learning Spaces, Nagapattinam

What if your best insights come not from a workshop, but from moulding clay or cooking over a fire?

Step into spaces where wisdom flows through doing and passed down patiently, purposefully, through generations.

Themes: Indigenous Knowledge • Sustainability • Learning by Making

Day - 3:
Build What’s Next

📍Chennai

Now, it’s your turn to create. Not just reflect.

Translate everything you’ve seen, challenged, and reimagined into prototypes that reshape the future of education.

Collaborate with bold minds, design without limits and leave not with ideas, but a solid plan.

Themes: Collaborative Prototyping • System Innovation • Future Roadmaps

FAQ's

I’m differently abled, will the zones be accessible?
Let us know your needs, we’ll do our best to accommodate.
Can I join for just one day?
This is a continuous 3-day journey. Partial attendance breaks the flow; we recommend committing fully.
I don’t have a background in design or education. Can I still join?
Yes! If you're curious, open to learning, and ready to get your hands dirty, you’ll fit right in.
Where will we stay during the conclave?
You’ll stay in clean, comfortable spaces near the coastal villages, close to where the learning happens. The stay is thoughtfully chosen to keep you connected to the context without compromising on rest and comfort.
What should I bring with me?
Comfortable clothes, sturdy footwear, curiosity, and an open mind. A detailed checklist will be shared after registration.
Whom can I contact if I have more questions?
Write to us at roots@vidhyavidhai.org
